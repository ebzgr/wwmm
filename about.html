About ESSEC CCM

The Communication, Culture & Media (CCM) program at ESSEC Business School is part of the "Comprendre & Changer le Monde" initiative. As future business leaders, students are guided by the 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development and the Planetary Boundaries Framework. This project represents the program's commitment to addressing global challenges through:

  • Understanding Complex Global Challenges
  • Developing Practical Solutions
  • Contributing to Sustainable Development
  • Creating Positive Social Impact
